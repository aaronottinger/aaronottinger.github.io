About Aaron

Aaron Ottinger teaches writing and rhetoric in the English Department at Highline College and the history of media in the department of Digital Technology and Cultures at Seattle University. His research is dedicated to interdisciplinary studies, with a focus on literature and science.

His current research examines how the alliance between Enlightenment mathematics and psychology influenced literary authors. He also investigates how measurement and quantitative thinking informed ideas regarding nature, empire, and the techno-global imagination.
