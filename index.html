About Aaron

Aaron Ottinger is a part-time writing instructor at Highline College and he teaches the history of media in the department of Digital Technology and Cultures at Seattle University. He completed his doctoral work in the English Department at the University of Washington (2016). His research focuses on science and literature. His most recent publications concentrate on the intersection of psychology and mathematics in British literature of the long eighteenth century. His work includes publications in Essays in Romanticism, the edited volume Romanticism and Speculative Realism (Bloomsbury 2019), and the forthcoming anthology The Palgrave Handbook to Literature and Mathematics.
